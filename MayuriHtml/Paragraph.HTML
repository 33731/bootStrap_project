Kavayitri Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University Jalgaon

University in Jalgaon, Maharashtra

Kavayitri Bahinabai Chaudhari North Maharashtra University is a university situated in Jalgaon, Maharashtra. Formely North Maharashtra University was established on 15 August 1990 after separating it from the parent University of Pune. In 2001, NAAC Accredited the prestigious 4 Star Status to the University.
